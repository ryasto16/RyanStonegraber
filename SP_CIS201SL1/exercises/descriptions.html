Definitions

Document Type Definition(DTD)
Identifies the version of HTML contained in a document
HTTP
Protocol used to define how messages are formatted and transmitted
ICANN
Non-profit organization responsible for coordinating the maintenance and procedures of several databases related to the namespaces of the internet
ftp
Protocol used for the tranfer of files between a client and server
WWW Consortium
Organization that works to develop web standards
TCP/IP
Protocol used for communication between computers
Tim Berners-Lee
Inventor of the Word Wide Web
Web 2.0
Second stage in development of the world wide web, characterized especially by the change from static webpages to dynamic
Creative Commons
A license type for an author who wants to give people the right to share, use, and build upon a work they have created
URL
A protocol for specifying addresses on the internet
Domain Name System(DNS)
The system by which the internet domain names and addresses are tracked and regulated
Section 508 of the Federal Rehabilitation Act
Requires federal agencies to make their electronic and information technology accessible to people with disabilities
